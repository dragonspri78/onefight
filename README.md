# OneFight Game Instructions

## Overview
OneFight is an exciting game where players battle opponents to become the ultimate champion. The goal is to defeat all opponents and claim victory.

## How to Play
1. **Start the Game:** Launch the game application.
2. **Select Your Character:** Choose from a variety of fighters, each with unique abilities.
3. **Fight Opponents:** Engage in battles, use strategies and special moves to defeat your opponents.
4. **Upgrade Your Skills:** Earn points after each fight to upgrade your character's skills and unlock new abilities.
5. **Claim Victory:** Defeat all opponents to win the game!

## Tips
- Experiment with different characters to find your favorite.
- Pay attention to your opponent's moves and learn how to counter them.
- Upgrade your character regularly for better chances of winning.
